Terms and Conditions -
  • As per the RBI Circular dated 27th March 2020 pertaining to COVID-19 – Regulatory Package, RBI has permitted lenders to allow its customers for Moratorium of upto three months on payment of all instalments falling due between March 1, 2020 and May 31, 2020.
  • The Moratorium facility is available to maximum of 3 EMI’s falling due between March 1, 2020 and May 31, 2020 and those who want to avail moratorium facility, can select whether they want to have moratorium of all 3 EMI’s or 2 EMI’s or only 1 EMI.
  • The 3 months Moratorium facility permitted by RBI shall not be construed as compulsion and customers can choose to make repayments as per pre-agreed repayment schedule of their requisite loan accounts.
  • This Moratorium facility is not available for customers whose first EMI is due on or after 1st June 2020.
  • Customer who want to avail Moratorium facility of 1 month – the next EMI will be due in April 2020, Customer who want to avail moratorium of 2 months – the next EMI will be due in May 2020 and Customer want to avail moratorium of 3 months – the next EMI will be due in June 2020.
  • The loan tenor will be extended as per the moratorium (period) option selected.
  • Moratorium facility shall not be anyway construed as interest waiver on EMIs due. The interest shall be calculated on the number of days EMI due during moratorium period. The interest on deferred payments shall be communicated to the customer separately and the same shall be payable by the customer availing Moratorium facility, along with their respective month’s EMI as per the moratorium period selected. The updated repayment schedule along with the moratorium charges will be communicated to you before your next EMI due date.
  • Moratorium will be provided to the customers whose consent is received through the DMI provided channel and before the cut off date of 3rd April.
  • For multiple active loan accounts eligible for taking Moratorium, consent against each loan account will be needed.

In case you have any other query or concern regarding moratorium scheme, please feel free to write to us at moratoriumsupport@dmifinance.in and we will get back to you at the earliest possible.
